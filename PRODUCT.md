# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

International B2B clients in interiors, automotive, aviation, hospitality, and product design who evaluate premium surface materials, SKU colors, technical information, cases, downloads, and contact details before speaking with CAMARI JAPAN.

## Product Purpose

CAMARI JAPAN v1 communicates premium material sensibility, Japanese restraint, Italian texture, and OEM/ODM capability while making materials, SKU colors, cases, and downloadable technical resources easy to browse across English and Japanese routes.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, tactile, precise. The brand should feel like a carefully edited material showroom: confident, restrained, textural, and commercially trustworthy without becoming generic corporate minimalism.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered ecommerce merchandising, generic SaaS landing-page patterns, decorative UI chrome that competes with material imagery, heavy inquiry funnels, and form-first contact flows. v1 contact is direct email, phone, and appointment information rather than a submission form.

## Design Principles

- Let materials carry the identity; UI chrome stays restrained.
- Preserve the CAMARI outline logo, wide letter spacing, and large serif typographic moments.
- Balance European material expressiveness with Japanese spatial restraint.
- Keep navigation, contact, downloads, and SKU exploration commercially clear for B2B buyers.
- Treat bilingual English/Japanese presentation as a first-class brand surface, not an afterthought.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Desktop, iPad, and mobile layouts must avoid text overlap, image collapse, and fixed-navigation blocking. Interactive SKU controls should remain keyboard-accessible, visible state changes should not require a page reload, and localized English/Japanese metadata and language alternates should be preserved.
